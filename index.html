Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Bitcoin mining and our services.

What is Bitcoin mining? +

Bitcoin mining is the process of adding transaction records to Bitcoin's public ledger (blockchain). Mining requires powerful computers that solve complex mathematical problems, and miners are rewarded with new bitcoins for their efforts.

How do your mining contracts work? +

Our mining contracts allow you to purchase mining power (measured in TH/s) for a specific period. We handle all the hardware setup, maintenance, and electricity costs, while you receive the mining rewards directly to your Bitcoin wallet.

When will I receive my mining rewards? +

We process mining rewards daily. Your Bitcoin earnings will be automatically sent to your designated wallet every 24 hours, with no minimum threshold required.

What happens if Bitcoin's price drops? +

Bitcoin's price fluctuations affect the dollar value of your mining rewards, but not the amount of Bitcoin you mine. We focus on maximizing your Bitcoin earnings, regardless of market conditions.

Is Bitcoin mining still profitable? +

Yes, Bitcoin mining can still be profitable, especially with our optimized operations using low-cost renewable energy and the latest mining hardware. Our calculator can help you estimate potential returns based on current conditions.

What happens when my contract ends? +

When your contract ends, you'll have the option to renew at the current rates or cash out. We'll notify you before your contract expires to discuss your options.
